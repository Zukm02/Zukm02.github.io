MLB Foul Ball Seating Optimizer

Class: Intro to Statistics (RStudio Project)

This project uses real-world foul ball data from Baseball Savant to help fans identify where to sit in MLB stadiums to either maximize their chances of catching a foul ball or minimize risk of injury. We explored ball exit velocity, type of hit, and zones of impact. This study was originally completed as part of my introductory statistics course using RStudio for data analysis and visualization.
