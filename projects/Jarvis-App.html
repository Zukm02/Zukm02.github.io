Jarvis is a custom Slack bot designed to interpret natural language commands using vectorization and classification techniques. Users can train Jarvis to recognize custom actions and then test it by inputting related phrases. The bot uses a simple state machine to switch between training and testing modes, making it both interactive and adaptable.
